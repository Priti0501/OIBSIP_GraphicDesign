Task 1 – Flyer Design

## Objective
To design an awareness flyer titled "Screen Time → Green Time" encouraging people to reduce screen time and spend more time in nature.

## Tools Used
 Canva and Pinterest

## Steps Performed
1. Selected eco-friendly theme and green color palette.
2. Added catchy headline "Screen Time to Green Time".
3. Included visual background with the help of Pinterest.
4. Maintained clean and readable layout.

## Outcome
A visually appealing awareness flyer promoting healthy digital habits and outdoor activities.
